About Me

As a passionate and dedicated student at UC Riverside, I've cultivated a strong foundation in English Literature, fostering my critical thinking, research skills, and creative writing abilities. My journey as a writer spans over a decade, with a focus on crafting engaging narratives for both children and young adults. My academic pursuits have been complemented by hands-on experience in various writing roles. I've contributed to educational visual novels, penned short stories and articles, and collaborated on game jam projects. Additionally, I've honed my skills as a volunteer tutor at College Corp, where I've gained valuable insights into the challenges faced by students and developed effective communication strategies. Beyond my academic endeavors, I've actively engaged in creative projects, including writing and directing my own short film. These experiences have allowed me to explore different storytelling mediums and develop a versatile skill set. My passion for writing, combined with my strong research and communication skills, make me a dedicated and adaptable content creator. I am eager to leverage my abilities to produce high-quality content that engages and informs audiences.
